## 📌 Project Summary

This project analyzes an E-commerce dataset using Microsoft Excel to evaluate business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

The analysis integrates Customer, Product, Sales, and Store datasets to generate actionable business insights through structured data cleaning, transformation, and aggregation.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into strategic insights that support revenue growth, customer retention, and operational optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Evaluate Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth
- Identify high-performing and slow-moving products
- Analyze customer loyalty contribution to revenue
- Compare gender-based purchasing behavior
- Study quarterly and seasonal sales trends
- Assess category-level and regional performance
- Evaluate store-type revenue contribution

## 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains four integrated tables:

- Customer (Demographics & Loyalty Data)
- Product (Category, Brand & Inventory Data)
- Sales (Transactional Revenue Data)
- Store (Regional & Channel Data)

The Sales table was used as the primary fact table, connected logically to customer, product, and store dimensions.

---

## 📊 Key Analytical Insights

### 🔹 Revenue Performance Analysis
- 2024 generated the highest revenue compared to 2023 and 2025.
- Indicates peak business performance and growth phase during 2024.
- Highlights need to investigate factors that drove 2024 growth.

---

### 🔹 Customer Loyalty Revenue Contribution
- Platinum tier customers generated the highest revenue.
- Higher loyalty tiers contributed disproportionately more revenue.
- Confirms that loyalty programs positively impact customer lifetime value.

---

### 🔹 Gender-Based Revenue Analysis
- Female customers contributed a higher share of total revenue.
- Suggests stronger purchasing frequency or higher average order value.
- Supports gender-focused marketing strategy optimization.

---

### 🔹 Product & Category Performance
- Sports category generated the highest overall revenue.
- Enables strategic inventory prioritization.
- Identifies revenue-driving product segments.

---

### 🔹 Quarterly & Seasonal Trends
- Identified seasonal fluctuations in sales.
- Notable revenue drop observed in Q4 2025.
- Supports improved forecasting and demand planning.

---

### 🔹 Store-Type Revenue Distribution
- Online and Flagship stores outperformed Outlet stores.
- Online channel showed consistent performance across periods.
- Supports continued investment in digital commerce channels.
